What can I do?
....make the web a better place, one site at a time.

My goal is to be a Grade A Certified JavaScript Guru! My core technologies are HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I can build a basic CRUD application with PHP & I like to play around with WordPress Development on the side. I soaked up as much as I could while in school and I still keep that "sponge" mentality today. I try to attend as many conferences, seminars, workshops, webinars and meetups as possible. There is so much information it's impossible to learn it all, but I'm definitely going to try!

I am smart enough to handle practicaly all of the requets a client can potentially make. I am wise enough to know when I can't. I concentrate on keeping my basic skill set sharp. I'll move on to the 'Wow Factor' once the foundation is immovable and firmly in place. I would say I'm somewhere between a 5 and a 6 on The 11 Phases of a Web Developer’s Career list (As Illustrated by Memes). I still have my 4's from time to time as well. Feel free to contact me for specific information regrding my competency level.
